Autonomous Serialization Data Integrity & Automation Defense

🚀 The Mission

Based in Rocky Top, TN, Sui-Generis specializes in securing the future of pharmaceutical automation through adversarial AI and real-time edge verification. Our goal is reducing serialization data friction and preventing unit quarantines in high-speed pharmaceutical packaging environments.

🛠️ Key Capabilities

  • Edge-Validation: Real-time verification of EPCIS data at the packaging line.
  • Adversarial Defense: Protection against agent-hijacking and prompt-injection in automated workflows.
  • Zero-Error Serialization: Using the FraudBeast framework to catch aggregation mismatches before they hit the warehouse floor.

🧪 Current Research & Development

  • FraudBeast: A cybersecurity simulation project testing defenses against evolving fraud attacks.
  • Modular Sentinel Framework: A Python-based agentic system for pharmaceutical supply chain security.
